Introduction To Our Solar System

Our solar system is a gravitationally bound system consisting of the sun, eight planets (Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune), dwarf planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and other icy bodies, all orbiting the Sun.
