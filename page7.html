Other Notable Substances

    Magnetic fields:

  • These fields are present throughout space, and they can interact with charged particles.

    • Neutrinos:

  • These are subatomic particles that interact very weakly with matter.

    • Dark Matter and Dark Energy:

  • These mysterious entities make up a large portion of the universe's mass and energy, but their nature is still unknown.

Asteroids

Asteroids lie mostly in the Asteroid Belt Between Mars and Jupiter. They are rocky, airless remnants left over from the early formation of our solar system.

Meteors

Meteors are the bright streaks of light in the sky caused by small space rocks (meteoroids) burning up as they enter Earth's atmosphere, often called "shooting stars". Meteoroids are those delving in the outer space while meteorites are those who landed on the land of earth.

Comets

Comets are icy, small Solar System bodies that orbit the Sun, often exhibiting long, bright tails as they approach the Sun, and are considered remnants from the formation of our solar system.
